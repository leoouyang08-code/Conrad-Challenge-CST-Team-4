Surveillance Program Of Threats

The Problem

Many errors in diesel generators used in unstable energy regions lead to fuel waste, financial loss, and vulnerability to natural disasters.

Our Solution

Our system uses machine learning to protect diesel generators in regions prone to natural disasters by analyzing real-time weather and meteorological data. This enables communities to predict disasters earlier, prepare effectively, and prevent generator failures.

Intended Impact

Our product will revolutionize the energy market as our automatic adjustment system for generators will become fuel efficient as it adjusts to the weather.
